## About

PAPERY is an open-source AI platform that transforms how people work with documents. Whether you're a researcher, student, analyst, or professional — PAPERY gives you superpowers to read, understand, query, and create documents with the help of AI.

Upload any document. Ask questions. Get insights. Generate reports. All in one place.

## Key Features

### 📄 Universal Document Support
- Read and process **all major formats**: PDF, DOCX, XLSX, PPTX, CSV, TXT, Markdown, and more.
- View documents directly in the UI with a built-in document viewer.

### 🔍 Intelligent Q&A & Research
- Ask questions about your documents and get accurate, cited answers.
- AI reads, comprehends, and cross-references across multiple documents.
- Extract key information, summaries, and insights automatically.
- Visual reference system — see exactly where answers come from in the original document.

### 📑 Citation & Reference
- Every AI response includes precise citations back to source material.
- Interactive reference panel — click a citation to jump to the exact location in the document.
- Export citations in standard academic formats.

### 🌐 Multi-Language Document Translation
- Translate entire documents across languages while **preserving the original structure and formatting**.
- Layout, tables, images, headers — everything stays in place, only the language changes.

### 🤖 Multi-Agent Research Workflows
- AI agents collaborate to research topics, gather information, and produce comprehensive outputs.
- Generate formatted documents that follow specific templates and rules:
  - Scientific reports, academic papers, business analyses
  - Custom templates provided by the user
  - Community templates from the marketplace
- End-to-end: from research question to polished, publication-ready document.

### ✏️ AI-Powered Document Editing
- AI can directly intervene in document structure — add, edit, or remove sections.
- Modify specific parts of a document while maintaining overall architecture and formatting.
- Collaborative editing: human guides, AI executes — with full control and undo.

### 💡 Insight Generation
- Automatic summarization and key takeaway extraction.
- Cross-document analysis to find patterns, contradictions, and connections.
- Topic clustering and knowledge graph generation.
